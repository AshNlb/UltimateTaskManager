Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to TaskFlow. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our task management application.

In short: We only collect the data necessary to provide you with our service. We don't sell your data, we don't share it with third parties, and we store it securely in Europe.

Information We Collect

When you use TaskFlow, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and encrypted password
  • Task Data: Tasks, buckets/projects, descriptions, due dates, and priorities you create
  • AI Assistant Usage: Questions you ask our AI assistant and the responses provided (limited to 10 questions per user per day)
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of when you create, update, or complete tasks

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the TaskFlow service
  • Allow you to access your tasks and data across devices
  • Provide AI-powered assistance for task management
  • Improve our service and fix bugs
  • Send you important service updates (if necessary)

Data Storage and Security

GDPR Compliance: All data is stored in secure servers located in Europe (Railway.app infrastructure).

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • Secure Connection: All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Database Security: Access to the database is restricted and monitored
  • Limited Access: Only the application owner has access to the database

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate TaskFlow:

  • Railway.app: Backend hosting and PostgreSQL database (EU servers)
  • Vercel: Frontend hosting and content delivery
  • OpenAI: AI assistant functionality (only when you use the Q&A feature)

When you use the AI assistant, your questions are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's data usage is governed by their privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing of your data

Cookies

TaskFlow uses minimal cookies and browser local storage to maintain your login session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Children's Privacy

TaskFlow is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:
